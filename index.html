Media Share Instructions

Use bits to share YouTube videos on stream. Each bit you send plays the video for 1 second.

How to Send:

To play a video from the beginning, send a message like this:

Cheer100 https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ

Plays the video from the start for 100 seconds.

To start at a specific time, send a message like this:

Cheer100 https://youtu.be/dQw4w9WgXcQ 2:34

Starts the video at 2:34 and plays for 100 seconds from that point.

To play a specific portion, use the following format:

Cheer100 https://www.youtube.com/shorts/lkCRPp7cnP8 2:34-2:35

This plays the section between 2:34 and 2:35 and loops until your bits run out (in this case, it repeats for 100 seconds).
